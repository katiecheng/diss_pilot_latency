Stanford AAA Lab

In the following activity, you will proceed through 3 stages:

  1. First, you will see 24 Swahili words paired with their English translations.
  2. Then, you will be asked to study each Swahili-English word pair--
    • 8 using whatever study method you would like
    • 8 using a strategy that you will be given
    • 8 using a different strategy that you will be given
  3. Finally, you will be quizzed on all 24 Swahili-English word pairs.
We will remind you of these instructions along the way.
